The Challenge

The Global Waste Problem

Businesses generate billions of tons of surplus food, packaging, and materials each year. Much of it ends up in landfills — not because it cannot be reused, but because there is no intelligent infrastructure connecting waste producers with recovery networks.

Without coordination, valuable materials are lost and sustainability goals remain difficult to measure.

The Solution

Introducing OrbisLoop

OrbisLoop is a digital infrastructure platform transforming global waste into high-value circular supply chains.

Unlike reactive recycling apps, OrbisLoop's Waste Intelligence Engine uses AI to predict surplus food and material waste before it occurs.

By combining a circular marketplace with a Smart Logistics Layer, OrbisLoop coordinates waste-to-resource recovery at scale.
